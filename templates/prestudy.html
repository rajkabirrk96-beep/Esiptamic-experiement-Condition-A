Welcome to the Investment Study

This study investigates how people make investment decisions with the assistance of AI-powered tools. You will complete 15 investment rounds, each taking about 1-2 minutes.

Total time: Approximately 35-40 minutes.

What you will do: In each round, you will see two stocks and an AI recommendation. You will decide how to split $1,000 between the two stocks.

Your participation is voluntary and your responses are anonymous. Data will be used for academic research only.

Investment Profile — 4 Quick Questions

Before we begin, please answer four short questions about your investment style. There are no right or wrong answers.
